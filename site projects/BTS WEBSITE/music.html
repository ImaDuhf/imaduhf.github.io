BTS

Discography

2 Cool 4 Skool

2 Cool 4 Skool is the debut single album of Korean group BTS, released on June 12, 2013. The album consists of 9 tracks in total, with 2 of them being hidden tracks “Path/Road” and “Skit: On The Start Line”. It also includes their very first cypher which was an intro to the cypher series, “Outro: Circle Room Cypher”.

2 Cool 4 Skool was the first of the album series known as the School Trilogy which would later include the albums 'O!RUL8, 2?'' and 'Skool Luv Affair'.

On May 21, 2013, Big Hit Entertainment launched a countdown clock on their official website to prepare for their debut and comeback promotions.

On June 12, 2013, BTS had a press conference and debut showcase as their unofficial debut performance into the music industry. On June 13, their official debut stage on Mnet's M Countdown began their promotion of 2 Cool 4 Skool on different music programs.

On May 26, 2013, a debut trailer was released on 1theK featuring hip-hop beats from the album's intro track and an edgy theme as the aimed image for the No More Dreams Era. The caption read “Finally BTS will make their debut!” The first teaser for the album was uploaded on June 6, 2013, and revealed that “No More Dream” would be the title track. The lead single later entered the Billboard World Digital Songs) at #14 on June 29, 2013 and stayed on it for 3 consecutive weeks.

The member line-up was confirmed in the concept photo of the album released on BTS' Facebook page.

The official track list, design, and details of the physical album were revealed on BTS' Twitter account.

An MV for the lead single (and debut song) “No More Dream” was released on Kpop YouTube channel 1theK on June 11, 2013 and a dance version was added on June 16. An MV for another track,“We Are Bulletproof Pt.2,” was released 1 month later for follow-up promotions.

O!RUL8,2?

O!RUL8,2? is the first mini-album by South Korean boy band BTS. It was released on September 11, 2013 as the follow-up extended play after 2 Cool 4 Skool. The album consists of ten tracks, with “N.O” as the lead single. The group later promoted “The Rise of Bangtan”, another track from the album. In 2013, the extended play was ranked fifty-fifth on Gaon's year end chart.

On August 27, 2013, following the release of their debut album 2 Cool 4 Skool, another countdown clock was launched on their official website along with a comeback trailer on YouTube to prepare and promote their next comeback, O! RUL8, 2?. On August 29, 2013, the track list and concept photos for the album were uploaded onto their official Twitter account and Facebook page. On their official shop website, the design and details of the physical album was also revealed. On September 4, 2013, Big Hit Entertainment released a concept trailer and a teaser for the title track N.O one day later. On September 8, 2013, an audio preview was dropped.

Their first comeback show for O!RUL8, 2? was on September 12, 2013 on Mnet's M Countdown which was then followed by performances on KBS's Music Bank, MBC's Music Core, SBS's Inkigayo and Arirang TV's Simply K-Pop, In November 2013, the album was further promoted with The Rise of Bangtan on music programs.

O!RUL8, 2? debuted at #4 on the Gaon Weekly Album Chart in the 2nd week of September 2013 and at #11 on the September Gaon Album Monthly Chart. It was on the Gaon Album Yearly Chart from 2013-2016 with a peak position at #55 in 2013, #92 in 2014, #87 in 2015, and #85 in 2016.

The Rap line (RM, J-Hope, SUGA) actually had 2 tracks in this, one being their first cypher and the other being Satoori Rap aka Paldogangsan which was actually the first song to bear the stamp “BTS” (includes members who would later become official members of the group) and released on August 17, 2011 to announce Big Hit's Hit It Audition.
